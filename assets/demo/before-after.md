# Before And After

## Before

A developer asks a strong technical question during a live AI session, gets a useful answer, and moves on. The reasoning helps in the moment, but the knowledge stays trapped in the chat. A week later, the same tradeoff comes up again, and the team has to reconstruct the answer from memory.

## After

The same conversation is classified as conceptual, rewritten into a clean question, summarized into a concise answer, tagged, and stored in `docs/daily/`. The insight is now visible in the repository, easy to review, easy to reuse, and no longer dependent on chat history.
